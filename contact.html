Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your delivery options?

We offer same-day delivery within the city and 2-3 day delivery for outstation orders. Free delivery is available for orders above ₹2000.

Do you offer tool rental services?

Yes, we provide rental services for heavy machinery and specialized tools. Contact us for rental rates and availability.

What is your return policy?
